Introduction

ScolairEx is a comprehensive school management system designed to streamline and automate daily administrative tasks in educational institutions. It provides an all-in-one platform for managing student records, attendance, grades, and communication between teachers, students, and parents. With user-friendly interfaces and secure data handling, ScolairEx enhances operational efficiency and improves the overall learning experience. Ideal for schools of all sizes, ScolairEx is a reliable solution for modern education management.

Getting Started

Welcome to ScolairEx! This guide will help you quickly get started with setting up and using the system’s core features. Below is an overview of the main modules included in ScolairEx:

1. Attendance Management

Efficiently track student and staff attendance, mark absences, and generate daily or monthly attendance reports. The module offers easy-to-use features for real-time monitoring and attendance analytics, ensuring a transparent and organized record-keeping system.

2. Hostel Management

Manage student accommodation details with ease. From room assignments and occupancy status to fee tracking and maintenance requests, the Hostel Management module helps administrators oversee all aspects of hostel operations smoothly.

3. Library Management

Organize and catalog books, track borrowings and returns, and manage overdue fines with the Library Management module. It provides tools for automated inventory updates, easy book searches, and seamless student interaction with library resources.

4. Financial Management

Handle financial transactions, including fee collection, payroll, and expense tracking, in a streamlined manner. This module supports detailed reports on revenue and expenses, helping administrators maintain transparency and financial accountability.

5. Inventory Management

Efficiently manage school assets, including classroom supplies, lab equipment, and other resources. The Inventory Management module helps track inventory levels, update stock information, and manage asset distribution across departments.

6. School Profile

Customize and manage essential information about the school, including the institution’s name, address, contact details, and logo. This module serves as a centralized hub for updating and maintaining the school’s public profile and core administrative details.

7. User Management

Securely manage user accounts, assign roles, and set access permissions for staff, students, and parents. The User Management module ensures that each user has access only to the information relevant to their role, enhancing security and personalization within ScolairEx.

8. Academic Management

Plan, organize, and monitor all academic activities within your institution. The Academic Management module enables the setup of courses, class schedules, curriculum planning, and examination management, offering a centralized hub for academic oversight and progress tracking.

9. Registration Management

Easily handle student admissions and enrollment with the Registration Management module. This feature streamlines the entire registration process—from new student intake to course registration—providing automated tools for managing application forms, admission status, and required documentation.

How is Registration Done

ScolairEx supports two primary registration types: Staff Registration, which facilitates the onboarding and management of educators and administrative personnel, and Student Registration, which streamlines the enrollment and maintenance of student records. Both registration processes ensure accurate and efficient user management within the system, catering to the distinct needs of staff and students.

Staff Registration

1. Navigate to the Staff Registration Page

From the main dashboard, select Registration Management on the left-side menu, then click on Staff Registration. This will open the Staff Registration form.

2. Fill in Personal Information

  • First Name: Enter the first name of the staff member.
  • Middle Name: Enter the middle name.
  • Surname: Enter the last name (surname).
  • Date of Birth: Select the date of birth from the date picker.
  • Gender: Choose the gender from the dropdown menu.

3. Enter Contact Information

  • Address: Provide the staff member's address.
  • Phone Number: Enter the contact phone number.
  • Email: Enter a valid email address for communication and login purposes.

4. Complete Employment Details

Add any additional employment-specific details required by the form. Fields may include job title, department, and other relevant employment information (these may vary based on setup).

5. Download or Upload CSV (Optional)

  • To batch upload staff data, you can Download CSV Template, fill it with staff details, and then Upload CSV to streamline registration.
  • Once you upload the CSV, you can Save CSV Data to complete the registration.

6. Save and Confirm

After filling out the form or uploading the CSV, review the details and click Save to register the staff member in the system.

7. Return to Dashboard

If you need to return to the main screen, use the Back button to navigate back to the dashboard.

Student Registration

1. Navigate to the Student Registration Page

  • From the dashboard, go to Registration Management on the left sidebar.
  • Select Student Registration to open the registration form.

2. Fill in Personal Information

  • First Name: Enter the student's first name in the provided field.
  • Middle Name (optional): Enter the student's middle name if applicable.
  • Surname: Enter the student's last name.
  • Date of Birth: Select the student's date of birth using the date picker.
  • Gender: Choose the student's gender from the dropdown.
  • Nationality: Select the student's nationality from the dropdown.

3. Fill in Address Information

  • Physical Address: Enter the student's address.
  • Student Type: Select the type of student from the dropdown (e.g., day scholar, boarder).

4. Fill in Parent Information

  • Parent Name: Enter the name of the parent or guardian.
  • Parent Occupation: Specify the parent's occupation.
  • Phone Number: Enter the contact number for the parent or guardian.
  • Email: Enter the parent’s email address.
  • Relationship: Choose the relationship between the student and the parent (e.g., mother, father, guardian).

5. Fill in Academic Information

  • Previous School Name: Enter the name of the school the student last attended.
  • Previous School Class Level: Select the student's previous class level.
  • Previous Combination (if applicable): Enter the subject combination from the previous school, if any.
  • Student Admission Number: Input the unique admission number for the student.

6. Upload Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate: Click on Upload Birth Certificate and select the file from your device.
  • Previous Report Cards: Click on Upload Previous Report Cards and upload the student’s report cards.
  • Medical Report: If applicable, click on Upload Medical Report and upload a file of the student’s medical report.

7. Provide Health Information

  • Do you have health insurance?: Select Yes or No from the dropdown based on whether the student has health insurance

8. Submit the Registration

  • Review all the information entered to ensure accuracy.
  • Click the Submit button at the bottom of the form to complete the registration process.

How is Academic Management Done

Academic Management

The Academic Management module provides a comprehensive system for overseeing various academic activities. It includes tools for managing classes, exams, and essential data, ensuring streamlined class operations, accurate exam assessments, and effective academic data management. This module supports a cohesive approach to student academic progress and institutional organization.

Class Management

Class Management As a sub model of the Academic model has other sub sections such as

  • class list
  • Timetable Allocation
  • Subject Class Allocation
  • Suspended Students
  • Special Attention Students
  • Student Class Allocation
  • Student Academic Status
  • Student Class Upgrading

Class List

The Class List page in the Scolairex Management System displays all registered classes, showing key details for each class, including the class name, class teacher, total number of students, invoice status, and academic year. This organized view allows administrators to quickly assess the status of each class, manage classes efficiently, and handle tasks such as updating class information or viewing class details.

How to create a new class

  • 1.Navigate to the Class Creation Page:
  • From the dashboard, go to the "Academic Management" section in the sidebar menu.
  • Select "Class Mgmt." and then click on "Create Class" to open the class registration form.
  • 2.Fill in the Class Details:
  • School Level:Select the appropriate level of the school (e.g., Primary, Secondary) from the dropdown menu.
  • Class Level:Choose the class level (e.g., Class 1, Class 2).
  • Class Name:Enter the name of the class to identify it within the system.
  • Class Teacher:Select the teacher responsible for the class from the list.
  • 3.Submit the Form:
  • After filling in all required fields, click the "Submit" button to save the new class.
  • Upon successful submission, the new class will be added to the class list, where it can be viewed and managed.

Timetable Allocation

Timetable allocation in an academic management system involves organizing and assigning class schedules for various subjects and activities. This process ensures that each class has a designated time slot and resources, making it easier for teachers and students to follow a structured learning schedule. It is a crucial aspect of academic planning, helping to avoid scheduling conflicts and maximizing resource utilization.

How to perform the Time Allocation

  • 1.Navigate to the Academic Management Section:
  • From the dashboard, locate the "Academic Management" section on the left-hand menu.
  • Click on the “Class Mgmt.” option to expand the available sub-options.
  • 2.Access the Timetable Allocation Page:
  • Under "Class Mgmt.," select “Timetable Allocation” to open the Timetable Allocation page.
  • 3.Add a New Timetable: On the Timetable Allocation page, click the green “+ Add New Timetable” button in the top right corner.
  • 4.Select Class for Timetable Allocation: In the new allocation form, choose the class you want to allocate a timetable to from the available dropdown options or list.
  • 5.Upload or Assign Timetable: Upload a PDF file containing the class's timetable or select an existing timetable if the system allows for preloaded templates.
  • 6.Save the Allocation: After selecting or uploading the timetable, click the “Save” or “Submit” button to finalize the allocation.
  • 7.Verify Allocation: After saving, the allocated timetable should appear in the list under "Timetable Class Listings" on the Timetable Allocation page. Each entry will have an option to view the PDF timetable and a "Remove" button if changes are needed.

Subject Class Allocation

Subject allocation is a crucial process in academic institutions, where subjects are systematically assigned to various classes and teachers. This process ensures that each class receives the appropriate subjects according to the curriculum and the teachers' expertise. Effective subject allocation helps in optimizing resources, maintaining a balanced workload for teachers, and providing students with a comprehensive educational experience. By clearly defining the subjects for each class, schools can enhance educational outcomes, improve teacher-student interactions, and ensure that all academic needs are met efficiently.

How is Subject Allocation Done

  • 1.Access the Subject Allocation Page:
  • Open the Scolairex Management System
  • Navigate to the "Academic" section from the main menu
  • Select "Subject Allocation" to open the subject allocation interface
  • 2.Add New Subject:
  • Click on the "Add Subject" button to open the "Add New Subject and Assign to Classes" dialog box
  • 3.Enter Subject Details:
  • In the "Subject Name" field, enter the name of the subject you wish to add.
  • 4.Select Classes:
  • In the "Select Classes" section, you will see a list of available classes.
  • Each row contains the following columns: Select (checkbox), School Level, Class Level, Class Name, and Teacher.
  • Check the boxes in the "Select" column for the classes you want to assign the new subject to.
  • 5.Review and Save:
  • Review the selected classes to ensure they are correct.
  • Click the "Save Subject" button to save the new subject and its class assignments.
  • If you need to cancel the operation, click the "Cancel" button.

Suspended Students

Managing suspended students efficiently is essential for maintaining discipline and ensuring a smooth academic environment. The Scolairex Management System provides a comprehensive platform to view, manage, and unsuspend students who have been temporarily removed from classes due to disciplinary actions.

Viewing Suspended Students

  • 1.Navigate to the "Suspended Students" Page
  • From the main dashboard, locate the "Academic Management" section on the left sidebar.
  • Click on "Suspended Students".
  • 2.View the List of Suspended Students:
  • The page will display a table listing all suspended students.
  • 3.Locate the Student:
  • Find the student you wish to unsuspend in the list. Each row represents a different student.
  • 4.Click the Unsuspend Button:
  • In the "Actions" column, there is a green button with a checkmark icon. Hovering over this button will display a tooltip that says "Unsuspend Student".
  • 5.Confirm the Action:
  • Click the green button to unsuspend the student. The system will update the student's status accordingly.

Special Attention Students

The "Special Attention Students" section of the Scolairex Management System is designed to manage and monitor students who require additional care and focus due to various reasons such as health conditions, behavioral issues, or special educational needs. This feature enables school administrators to document and keep track of such students efficiently, ensuring that their specific requirements are addressed promptly. It includes options to view, filter, and remove students from the list if needed.

  • 1.Navigate to the Section
  • From the left-hand menu, locate the Academic Management section.
  • Click on the Special Attention Students link to open the relevant page.
  • 2.Viewing Students with Special Attention
  • Upon accessing the page, you will see a table labeled "Special Attention Students".
  • The table includes the following details for each student (Student name , Admission number)
  • 3.Filtering the list
  • Use the search bar at the top-right of the table to filter specific records.
  • The table will dynamically display matching results as you type.

Class Allocation

The Class Allocation feature in the Scolairex Management System enables administrators to assign students to specific classes based on their academic levels or requirements. This process ensures that students are properly grouped into their respective classes, streamlining academic management and ensuring an organized learning environment. It allows for efficient management of class assignments and provides flexibility in updating or changing class allocations as needed.

  • 1.Navigate to the Class Allocation Section
  • From the left-hand menu under Academic Management, locate the Class Mgmt. subsection.
  • Click on Class Allocation to open the relevant page.
  • 2.Select the Action
  • Under the Select Action dropdown
  • Choose the action you wish to do (Assign to Class, Update Class)
  • 3.Select the Class
  • In the Select Class dropdown
  • Choose the class to which you want to allocate or reassign students.
  • 4.Retrieve Students
  • Click the Retrieve Students button.
  • The system will display a list of students eligible for the selected action and class in the Students Listings table.
  • 5.Select Students
  • Use the checkboxes in the Students Listings table to select individual students.
  • 6.Confirm Allocation
  • After selecting the students, click the appropriate Action Button (e.g., Assign or Save) to finalize the allocation.

Academic Status Management

The Academic Status Management feature in the Scolairex Management System allows administrators to track and manage the academic status of students for a given academic year. This functionality ensures proper categorization of students based on their current academic standing, such as active, promoted, graduated, or any other relevant status. Managing academic status is essential for keeping accurate records and facilitating smooth academic operations.

  • 1.Navigate to the Academic Status Section
  • On the left sidebar menu, click Academic Management to expand the options.
  • Select Academic Status from the dropdown.
  • 2. Apply Filters to Retrieve Students
  • Use the Select Filters section to specify:
  • Academic Year: Choose the appropriate academic year from the dropdown menu.
  • Academic Status: Select the status you want to filter by (e.g., Active, Graduated, Suspended).
  • Click the View Students button to retrieve the list of students matching the selected criteria.
  • 3. Review the Student List
  • The Students Listings table will display the following details for each student:
  • Admission Number
  • Full Name
  • Gender
  • 4. Perform Actions on the Student List
  • Use the options provided to manage students’ academic status :
  • Select Individual Students: Click the checkbox next to each student to select them.
  • Bulk Selection: Click Select All to apply actions to all students in the list.
  • Assign or update the academic status as needed (e.g., promoting students to a new academic year, marking students as graduated).

Class Upgrading

Class upgrading is an essential process in the Scolairex Management System that allows administrators to promote students from their current class level to the next academic class. This functionality ensures a smooth transition for students as they progress through their academic journey. It helps maintain accurate academic records and keeps the system organized for both administrators and teachers.

  • 1.Navigate to the Upgrade Students Section
  • On the left sidebar menu, locate and expand the Academic Management section.
  • Click on Upgrade Students to access the class upgrading interface.
  • 2.Select Academic Year and Class
  • Use the provided dropdown menus to specify the following:
  • Academic Year: Select the academic year for which you want to perform class upgrades.
  • Class: Choose the current class whose students you want to upgrade.
  • Click the Retrieve Students button to fetch the list of students in the selected class.
  • 3.Review the List of Students
  • The system will display the list of students in a table with the following details:
  • Student Name
  • Parent Contact
  • Current Class Name
  • If no students are available for the selected class, the table will show a message indicating that no students match the selected criteria.
  • 4.Select Students to Upgrade
  • You can either :
  • Select individual students by checking the box next to their names.
  • Use the Select All checkbox to choose all students in the list.
  • Ensure that the selected students meet the criteria for class promotion.
  • 5.Confirm and Perform the Upgrade
  • Click the Upgrade or Confirm button (depending on the system configuration).
  • A confirmation dialog will appear—review the details and confirm your action.

Exam Management

The Exam Management module in the Scolairex Management System is a comprehensive tool designed to streamline the entire process of handling student assessments. It provides administrators and teachers with an intuitive platform to manage exam-related tasks efficiently. From entering scores to generating reports, this module ensures accuracy, transparency, and accountability in academic evaluations.

Exam Score Entry

  • 1.Navigate to the Score Entry Module
  • On the dashboard, under "Academic Management," select Exam Mgmt and click on Score Entry.
  • 2.Select the Desired Class
  • In the Score Entry Point page, a list of classes is displayed.
  • Identify the class for which you wish to input scores.
  • 3.Download the CSV Template
  • Locate the "Download" button in the Action column for the corresponding class.
  • Click the Download button to obtain the preformatted CSV template.
  • 4.Fill in the Exam Scores
  • Open the downloaded CSV file using a spreadsheet application (e.g., Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets).
  • Enter the scores for each student in the appropriate columns provided in the template.
  • Ensure accuracy in student details and scores to avoid errors during upload.
  • 5.Save the Completed Template
  • After entering all the scores, save the file in the same CSV format to maintain compatibility with the system.
  • 6.Upload the Completed File
  • Return to the Score Entry page in the system.
  • Click the "Upload" option or similar functionality provided.
  • Select the saved CSV file from your device and upload it.
